Safety Concerns and Liabilities



Concerns


Deep Water Malfunction

Always be cautious when controling the Aquabot and make sure you know how deep you're sending it underwater.

Sensor Malfunction

Be mindful of your robot's capabilities and make sure to go over the hardware diagnostics. When you're sending your robot underwater, make sure there is a sufficent amount of energy.




Wire Disconnection

Remmember to check if the wires are all connected. Loose connections could lead to a failure.

Location Disconnection

There could be a problem with the location of the Aquabot. Take precautions when sending the robot underwater.





Safety


Battery Safety

Checking the aquabot battery is a must. A bad battery could cause a wildlife disaster underwater.

Wildlife Interaction

Sometimes things malfunction and don't go as planned. Interactions between the aquabot and wildlife may not go well. In some cases it could harm the wildlife.





Liabilities


Warranty

If something was to happen to the robot without previous damage then we will take care of it. Malfunctions will be applicable to the warranty. This is a lifetime warranty.

Aquabot Damage

Not using the aquabot properly will lead to expenditure toward new parts. Be careful while using the aquabot.
