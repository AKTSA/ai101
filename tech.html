Internet of Things

What is it?

Many devices connected through a shared network that share and process data. Devices like appliances, electronics, sensors, etc.

Why is it important?

One of the technologies with high demand. Iot has solved many solutions for companies like Google and Netflix. Internet of Things is a big technology in our lives. Devices like the Amazon Echo and Google Home use it. Iot plays a major role in entertainment, security, and connectivity.

Big Data

What is it?

In simple terms, Big Data is basically analyzing large amounts of data and finding patterns/trends within the data. This could lead to predictions and assumptions.

Why is it important?

A technology being used in companies such as Amazon and Netflix, Big Data has changed the way we interpret data. Big data provides vital data for companies to analyze and make use of.

Augmented Reality

What is it?

Augmented Reality is simply augmenting virtual elements into the real world. This way you could place objects and see how it looks.

Why is it important?

AR is taking a big impact in both the ecommerce and gaming. Many furniture/home improvement companies are moving towards AR, so they can enhance their way of presenting their products.
