The built design and high quality parts of the Aquabot allows for transportation of goods and products of up to 5,000 lbs, higher than the maximum loads of several trucks and vans. The Aquabot SDK also includes a complex navigation software apt for calculating directions to any US address.

The Aquabot's powerful underwater intellisense gives it the ability to quickly scan any large setting for any incidents or emergencies. The Aquabot can also perform most medical operations under pressure, and it has been tested in several simulations and scenarios.

Not satisfied with your recent athletic performance? With the Aquabot's Sports Mode, you can practice any sport as well as train with common gym equipment. Not only does the Aquabot physically function as a trainer, but it also provides accurate feedback as to how you can improve your technique.

With the built-in Aquabot VR headset, you can perform any virtual task without the cost and risks of physically performing it. This may include watching a sports game, practicing a sport, or simply playing a game.
