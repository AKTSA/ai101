Uses of the Aquabot

Safety

Why risk a life when lifeguarding at a pool or beach? The Aquabot has sophisticated analysis programs to constantly survey a large population. In the event of an emergency, the Aquabot has access to expansive medical databases that can be applied to any situation.

Recreational Activities

The Aquabot can benefit any water sport enthusiasts, including swimmers, divers, and surfers, with its training programs. Supplemented by Machine Learning, the Aquabot is constantly improving at every sport, allowing users to develop to their full potential in any underwater sport.

Deep-Sea Research

There is an abundance of undiscovered life waiting in the depths of the ocean, but many researchers lack the advanced technology needed to explore at those depths. With Aquabot, researchers can take advantage of its built in Oxygen converter as well as its expansive network powered by Artifical Intelligence to aid their deep-sea endeavors.
