Concerns


Deep Water Malfunction

Always be cautious when controling the Aquabot and make sure you know how deep you're sending it underwater.

Sensor Malfunction

Be mindful of your robot's capabilities and make sure to go over the hardware diagnostics. When you're sending your robot underwater, make sure there is a sufficent amount of energy.




Wire Disconnection

Remmebr to check if the wires are all connected. Loose connections could lead to a failure.

Location Disconnection

There could be a problem with the location of the Aquabot. Take precautions when sending the robot underwater.
