What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction. Particular applications of AI include expert systems, speech recognition, and machine vision. To learn more about the applications of AI, see our uses page.

What are the benefits?

Because of its ability to recursively self-improve, AI can develop a superintelligence that can help the human race in a multitude of ways. From eradicating events from war to poverty, the creation of AI might be the biggest event in human history. Furthermore, the ability to perform tasks without the articulation of our own knowledge allows AI-powered systems to perform tasks on their own.
